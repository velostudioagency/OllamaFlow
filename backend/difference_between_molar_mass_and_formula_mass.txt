Key differences between Molar Mass and Formula Mass:\n\n1. Units: Molar mass is expressed in units such as grams per mole (g/mol), while formula mass is typically expressed in atomic mass units (amu).\n2. Number of moles: Molar mass takes into account the number of moles, whereas formula mass does not.\n3. Precision: Molar mass provides a more precise measurement due to its consideration of the Avogadro's constant.
